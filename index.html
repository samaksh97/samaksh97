Time Series Clustering for grouping stock prices during Covid

The onset of the Covid pandemic brought a profound shock to the financial markets in early 2020. Major indices and stocks took a resounding hit, with SP500 showing a decline of about 34% from its February high to its March 23 bottom. Following the initial shock, though, many stocks exhibited a strong recovery on the back of interest rate cuts by the Fed and other government policies. This analysis aims to uncover groups among the S&P 500 stocks in their drop and rebound trajectory shown during Covid and identify the drivers behind them.
